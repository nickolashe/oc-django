How transactions work

To make it as easy as possible to accept Currents, nothing changes at point of sale. Customers will pay full price and provide openCurrents with proof of purchase.

At the end of the month, we will invoice you the total amount discounted to customers, then reimburse customers and deduct their Currents. For every $10 discounted, you will have the choice to receive 1 Current or claim a $10 charitable donation.

Example: If you accepted a 20% discount on $100 in sales, you would receive $80 and either 2 or a $20 writeoff.
