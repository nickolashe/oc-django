Our story

Danny, Nicko, and Max cofounded openCurrents in 2017 to give the world a way to value social action.
We believe that the time we invest for the greater good is extremely valuable and that society as a whole needs more of its awesomeness.
The evolution begins from the heart of the Lone Star State: Austin, Texas.
