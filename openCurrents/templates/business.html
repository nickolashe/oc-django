Who benefits?

Everyone.

Volunteers can spend earned Currents with participating businesses or can "give twice" by donating Currents back to organizations.

openCurrents enables social organizations to recruit quality, committed volunteers and raise additional funds.

By accepting Currents, businesses can meaningfully engage their target audience and value their customers' favorite cause. Returns may increase as Currents become more valuable.
